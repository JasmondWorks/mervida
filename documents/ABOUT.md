About GFO FOODS LIMITED

GFO FOODS LIMITED is a premium food and wellness company based in Lagos, Nigeria, committed to providing natural, high-quality, and clean-label food products. Founded by Mrs. Victoria Olorede, the company combines innovation with tradition to deliver products that are both healthy and flavorful.

Our mission is to enhance the well-being of individuals and families by supplying food ingredients, natural seasonings, and wellness products that are safe, nutritious, and convenient.

With the introduction of Mervida, our natural seasoning and wellness brand, GFO FOODS LIMITED expands its promise of quality to include herbal and natural personal care solutions, empowering individuals to live healthier and more vibrant lives.

Quality products made with integrity

Safe, clean-label ingredients

Solutions that meet the needs of modern households and small businesses
